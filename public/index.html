About

Welcome to Suchi Art Creation, where tradition meets creativity and every piece of art tells a timeless story.

With over 20 years of experience in the world of fine arts and crafts, the proprietor of Suchi’s Art Creations, Suchi Thapar, has dedicated her journey to preserving and celebrating the beauty of traditional Indian art forms.

Having practiced a variety of traditional Indian and folk arts, such as Tanjore, Gurjari, Madhubani, Pichwai, Warli, and Lippan art, she brings her passion and enthusiasm into every project. Along with Indian art forms, she has a command on Acrylic painting, ceramics, resin art, and various other craft techniques, which she often practices as both profession and hobby.

At Suchi Art Creation, art is not just made; it is lovingly brought to life. We invite you to explore our collection and experience the charm of handcrafted excellence, where every brushstroke carries creativity, intimacy, and grace.
